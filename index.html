WHO WE ARE

Engineering the Future
of Underground Transit

The Boring Illini is an interdisciplinary student engineering team at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, building a fully electric Tunnel Boring Machine (TBM) from the ground up to compete on the world stage.

Founded in June 2025 and supported by the Grainger College of Engineering, our team brings together students from mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, and business — united by one goal: design, build, and operate a world-class TBM.

We compete in The Boring Company's Not a Boring Competition, a global contest challenging university teams to rethink how tunnels are built. Our machine design is driven by four core principles: Simplicity. Reliability. Modularity. Low-Cost.

THE COMPETITION

Not a Boring
Competition

Hosted by The Boring Company — Elon Musk's tunneling and infrastructure startup — the Not a Boring Competition challenges university teams worldwide to design and build the fastest, most efficient small-scale tunnel boring machines.

Teams must bore a tunnel of specified dimensions as quickly as possible, scoring on speed, alignment, and engineering elegance. It's the Formula 1 of underground engineering — and the Boring Illini are in it.

OUR STRUCTURE

Five Subteams.
One Machine.

◎
Cutterhead
Blade design, structural FEA, gearbox integration, bearing assembly, and jig manufacturing. The face that does the cutting.
Lead: Milena Savić
⬆
Propulsion
Structural chassis design, screw jack thrust system, FEA simulations, roller brackets, and anchored structure integration.
Lead: Vedant Thakar
◈
Muck Removal
Auger conveyor design, soil mechanics, ACP soil conditioning system, material transport flow analysis and testing.
Lead: Alexander Xie
⚡
Electronics
480V power distribution, VFD selection and configuration, E-stop logic, ground fault protection, electrical enclosures, RS-485/Modbus control network, Python GUI telemetry dashboard, and sensor integration.
Lead: Vihaansh Majithia
◐
Sponsorship & Marketing
Industry partnerships, fundraising, media, competition logistics, merchandise, and organizational outreach.
Lead: Tom Hong
